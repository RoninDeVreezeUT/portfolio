Ronin de Vreeze

Hi! I'm Ronin, a 20 year old Creative Technology student passionate about a lot of different things. I love combining different subgenres of art, video, music and techonlogy to create unique projects. I invite you to take a look at my work down below.

More about me, I currently live in Amsterdam and work parttime as a line cook at a old atmospheric cafe/restaurant in the city center. Not only at work, but also in my free time I like to cook for myself and search for new music genres during it. When I have the time I also like to rock climb once in a while with friends.

My main interests are related to the combination of physical technology and digital technology. Projects that combine the real world (retro tech / smart devices / displays) with digial software (audiovidual software / interfaces / websites) tend to pique my interests. Enjoy!
